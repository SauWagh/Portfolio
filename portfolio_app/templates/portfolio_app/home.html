About

Hi, I’m Saurabh — a Python/Django Full-Stack Developer who loves building things that actually work and actually look good. I turn ideas into fast, scalable, and clean web applications using Django, REST APIs, PostgreSQL, SQL, JavaScript, and modern UI frameworks.

But I don’t stop at full-stack development — I also enjoy giving applications a brain . With AI & Machine Learning (Scikit-Learn, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, seaborn), I build smart features like predictions, automation tools, and data-driven insights that make projects feel more alive.

I’m the kind of developer who’ll fix your backend logic, design a smooth UI, optimize the database, and still have the energy to train an ML model — all in the same sprint.

I learn fast, think creatively, and love solving problems with simple, elegant code. If you’re looking for someone who brings fresh ideas, full-stack skill, and AI-powered thinking, I’d love to build something amazing with you.

Let’s create something impactful.
